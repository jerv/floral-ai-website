Introduction

Welcome to Floral AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account information (email, name) when you sign up
  • Apple ID information when using Apple Sign-In
  • Payment information processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system

Usage Information

  • Flower arrangement preferences and selections
  • Generated images and arrangements you create
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device information and app performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI flower arrangement services
  • Process your subscription and payment transactions
  • Save and sync your created arrangements across devices
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send important app updates and service notifications

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Generated arrangements and user preferences are stored on secure cloud servers to enable synchronization across your devices.

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

  • Apple Sign-In: For secure authentication
  • In-App Purchases: For subscription and payment processing
  • iCloud: For data synchronization (optional)

AI Services

  • OpenAI DALL-E: For generating flower arrangement images
  • Your arrangement preferences are sent to generate images but are not stored by OpenAI

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Correct or update your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Export your created arrangements

GDPR Compliance: If you're in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to be forgotten.

Children's Privacy

Floral AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of the app after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

Email: floralai.app@gmail.com

Support: Support provided on a best-effort basis

Apple App Store Compliance

This app complies with Apple's App Store Guidelines regarding:

  • In-App Purchase requirements for digital content
  • User privacy and data collection standards
  • Subscription management and cancellation policies

For subscription management, users can cancel or modify subscriptions through their Apple ID account settings.
